About Resume Scanner

Welcome to Resume Scanner, an AI-powered tool that helps applicants analyze their resume and cover letter for job suitability.

Our Purpose

Resume Scanner uses machine learning to evaluate your resume and cover letter, providing insights into how well you match a specific job role.

How It Works

  • Upload your resume and cover letter.
  • Select one or multiple job roles.
  • Our AI model analyzes your documents and provides a suitability score.

Why Use Resume Scanner?

  • Get instant AI-powered insights.
  • Improve your chances of landing your desired job.
  • Tailor your resume and cover letter for better job matches.
